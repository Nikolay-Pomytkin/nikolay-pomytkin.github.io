Questions & Answers

What’s the most fun thing you’ve done so far this year?

Over the winter break, I went to Texas to visit my older sister (who lives/works in Austin) and we went on a road trip exploring different parts of Texas and New Mexico. I think one of the best (and most fun) parts of that experience was visiting the Carlsbad caverns (in NM). The Carlsbad Caverns are really complex, featuring over 100 different caves, and it was really interesting to see the kinds of ancient formations that you can see throughout the caves (stalactites and stalagmites).
